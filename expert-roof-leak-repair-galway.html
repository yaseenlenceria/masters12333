Common Roof Leak Types We Repair

🏠 Slate & Tile Leaks

Cracked, slipped, or missing slates and tiles. Wind damage, age deterioration, and poor installation repairs.

  • • Thermal imaging identifies exact locations
  • • Individual slate/tile replacement
  • • Lead flashing and pointing repairs

🌧️ Flat Roof Leaks

Felt, EPDM, and membrane roof leaks. Ponding water, splits, and junction failures.

  • • Membrane integrity testing
  • • Drainage system assessment
  • • Permanent waterproof repairs

🔧 Flashing Leaks

Chimney, valley, and ridge flashing failures. Lead work deterioration and sealant failures.

  • • Complete flashing replacement
  • • Lead welding and soldering
  • • Weather-resistant sealants

💧 Gutter Overflow Leaks

Blocked gutters causing overflow into roof structure. Joint failures and improper falls.

  • • Gutter realignment and repair
  • • Downpipe clearing and replacement
  • • Leaf guard installation
