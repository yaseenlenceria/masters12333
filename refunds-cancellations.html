Refunds & Cancellations

Our fair and transparent refund policy

Last Updated

August 2025

At The Masters Group, we aim to provide high-quality services. This policy explains refunds and cancellations for our roofing, cleaning, painting, and paving services.

1. Cancellations

24+ Hours Notice

Customers may cancel services with 24 hours' notice without charge.

Less than 24 Hours

If less than 24 hours' notice is given, a call-out fee may apply.

2. Refunds

Services Not Started

If services have not started, full refunds will be issued.

Partially Completed

If a project is partially completed, refunds will be calculated based on work done and materials used.

Completed Jobs

Completed jobs are non-refundable but covered by service warranties where applicable.
