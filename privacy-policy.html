Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us - GDPR compliant data protection

Last Updated

August 2025

At The Masters Group (RoofMasters, DirtMasters, PaintMasters, and PavingMasters), your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with Irish and EU data protection law, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

Name, email address, phone number, address, and service-related details provided by you.

Website Usage Data:

Information about how you use our website, including cookies and analytics.

Communication Data:

Records of email, phone calls, and form submissions for customer service purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide roofing, cleaning, painting, paving, and landscaping services.
  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotes.
  • To send booking confirmations, invoices, and updates.
  • To improve our services and website functionality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may only share data with:

  • Our trusted service partners operating under The Masters Group.
  • Legal authorities, if required by Irish law.

4. Data Security & Retention

We use secure systems to protect your information. Your data will be retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by Irish law.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission Ireland.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: info@themastersgroup.ie
